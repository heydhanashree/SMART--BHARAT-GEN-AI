# SMART--BHARAT-GEN-AI
Smart Bharat AI – AI-Powered Civic Companion 🇮🇳

Smart Bharat AI is an AI-powered civic assistance platform developed to make government services more accessible, simple, and user-friendly for every citizen. The application acts as a digital companion that helps users find information about government schemes, understand eligibility criteria, access required documents, and receive instant AI-based guidance.

The platform also enables citizens to report public issues such as potholes, water supply problems, electricity failures, streetlight faults, garbage collection issues, and sanitation concerns. Users can track the status of their complaints through an integrated complaint tracker, promoting transparency and better communication between citizens and local authorities.

To make the platform inclusive and accessible, Smart Bharat AI provides multilingual support in English, Hindi, and Marathi, allowing users to interact in their preferred language. The application includes an intuitive interface, voice input support, AI-powered chatbot assistance using the Google Gemini API, a government services directory, document guidance, and real-time complaint management.

Built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Google Gemini AI, Smart Bharat AI focuses on creating a seamless digital governance experience. Its primary objective is to bridge the gap between citizens and government services by providing quick access to information, encouraging civic participation, and promoting transparent, efficient, and smart governance.
